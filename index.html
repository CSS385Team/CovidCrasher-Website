About the Game

Currently, there is only one way to recover from COVID-19. Unfortunately, it's very difficult and dangerous to undergo the healing process... A doctor is in the pursuit of saving his severely ill patient. He is shrunken to microscopic size and is tasked with traversing through their respiratory system. Starting from the nasal cavity, down the trachea and finally into the lungs, fighting the virus along the way using unconventional tools. Embark on a treacherous journey, in hopes of eliminating the root cause of the disease.
